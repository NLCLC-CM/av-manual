# Saturday

This entire process can be done by 1 person. There are no outstanding prerequisites.

## Arriving at your station

AV operators typically arrive at their stations 30 minutes before start, up to 10 minutes before start.

## Procedure

### Start-up

- Open the cabinet containing audio equipment
- As per instructions, turn on the main audio equipment
- Turn on smaller amp for lobby and nursery
    - (Optional) Make sure that the amp in the nursery is powered
- Place microphones (3 wired, 1 wireless) onto stage
- Check sound for all 4 microphones
    - Make sure that the wireless microphone has enough battery power, and then turn it off to conserve
      power
- Starting presenting the powerpoint
    - Turn on overhead projector and TV/monitor
    - Download this Saturday's powerpoint
    - Start presenting the powerpoint
- (If required) Help worship team with their practice by balancing levels appropriately

### Shut-down

- Bring all levels down (or mute)
- Remove microphones from stage and place in cabinet
- Turn off overhead projector and TV/monitor
- As per instructions, turn off the main audio equipment
- Close and lock both doors on the cabinet
- Return the key to the office

**Note.** Avoid keeping the remote for the projector/TV or the keys to the cabinet in your pockets.
There have been instances where they are taken outside of church. **You don't want to be the one called
to return them at some inconvenient time.** These incidents will be imprinted into CM AV team
living memory for at least a decade. It is very hilarious.
